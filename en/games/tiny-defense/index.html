THE GAME

Prepare by day.
Survive the night.

Gather resources in the forest, grow your village and army, and defend your castle from the monsters that arrive each night.

Tiny Defense brings gathering, unit placement, and auto-battles to a one-handed portrait screen. Every small choice you make during the day shapes your chances of surviving the night.

DAY & NIGHT

A day's choices
shape the next night.

01

DAY / PREPARE

Gather by day

Collect wood, stone, iron, and food, then invest them where they matter most. The order in which you gather and upgrade lays the foundation for your defense.

  • Four resources and tools
  • Build and grow your village
  • Scout for the next night
02

NIGHT / DEFEND

Defend by night

Command spearmen, archers, and monks, and build towers. Hold back the oncoming hordes and push deeper into the night.

  • Drag to command your units
  • Tower and bombardment tactics
  • An endless survival challenge that grows tougher

BUILD YOUR RUN

Defend your castle
your way, every run.

From your unit composition to heroes, relics, and seasons, new choices open up the longer you survive.

01

Shape your defense line

Send spearmen, archers, and monks exactly where you need them, then combine them with two tower types to hold off dense enemy waves.

02

One-on-one boss duels

Every fifth night, chain the on-screen commands to face the boss head-on as your hero, or take it on alongside your army.

03

Survive beyond the seasons

Combine relics, hero traits, and spirits, then take on a deeper survival run through spring, summer, fall, and winter.
